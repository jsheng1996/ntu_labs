# CZ3005 Artificial Intelligence

Computer / software engineers are involved in effective and efficient building of knowledge agent systems that satisfy the
requirements of users; possibly software for intelligent embedded and intelligent information systems. General awareness
of theory, knowledge, and practice in all phases of the knowledge based systems and representation techniques for
problem solving are necessary for those of you who wants to get into the field artificial intelligence. These are advanced
intelligent systems that are finding wide spread applications in finance, banking, manufacturing industries.
